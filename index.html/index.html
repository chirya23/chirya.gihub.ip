Biography

Vitaly Olegovich Tsal (November 19, 1990) in Vinnitsa, better known by the pseudonyms "Arthas" and "better known by the pseudonyms "Arthas" and "Papich" Ukrainian streamer, letsplayer, video blogger and youtuber.A former Twitch streamer, he currently broadcasts on YouTube and Trovo. Initially known for his high level playing of Dota 2 and his eccentric behavior during broadcasts, he soon became one of the most recognizable and popular streamers in the CIS. Vitaly had no friends in his childhood, only acquaintances, but despite this, considers his childhood purely happy, because he was not obliged to anyone and lived as a child carefree. In parallel with the wide popularity, according to the streamer himself, he intentionally leads a misanthropic lifestyle, which does not prevent him from occasionally distributing videos with appeals to help seriously ill children, as well as organizing charity streamers to raise funds to help the needy, an animal shelter and victims of the missile attack on the center of Vinnitsa.
